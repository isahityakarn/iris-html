Information

Iris Computers helps businesses fully realize the promise of technology™—helping them maximize the value of the technology that they make, sell, or use. With its vast national infrastructure and focus on IT Hardware Distribution Technology Solutions, Iris Computers enables business partners to operate more efficiently and successfully in the markets they serve.No other company delivers as broad and deep a spectrum of IT Hardware Technology and Logistics services to businesses in India.

3 decades of excellence and much more, Iris Computers is the most trusted IT Hardware distribution company that creates opportunities for enterprise advancement for its channel partners through aggressive market development and continuous improvement through agility. Iris Computers amplifies the value of its position at the intersection of the vendors, reseller, and retailer partners by customizing and delivering IT Hardware Solutions for industry verticals, business to business customers, and commercial needs.

As one of India’s leading end-to-end technology distributor, we put all our focus on our customer’s needs and goals with the objectivity of a true strategic partner. With reach, competence and resources that span the technology gamut, we are a vital link in the IT channel, delivering the acumens and optimized solutions our channel partners depend on to compete in the market today with the technology of the future.
