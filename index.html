A Little About Us

Iris Computers, 25 years old company, is a brand known for its flexibility & fast delivery and represents global brands viz. Hewlett Packard, Dell, LG, Acer, Ruckus, APC, Numeric, Consul, Inspur, Wacom among others. We have presence across the country at 27 locations with our own offices and warehouses equipped to handle real-time transactions catering to the needs of over 3000 + Channel partners. We take pride in our association with global brands, dedicated Channel partners and customers, which won us many awards and recognitions year after year.

As one of India's leading end-to-end technology distributor, we put all our focus on our customer's needs and goals with the objective of a true strategic partner. With reach, competence and resources that span the technology gamut, we are a vital link in the IT channel, delivering the acumens and optimized solutions our channel partners depend on to compete in the market today with the technology of the future.
